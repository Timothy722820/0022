Thailand Festival

Thailand, formerly known as Siam, is located in the heart of Indochina between China and India. The whole country respects Buddhism. For centuries, it has been a gathering place for religions, cultures and multiple ethnic groups in Southeast Asia. Thailand covers an area of ​​approximately 500,000 square kilometers. The population is about 60 million. Thailand is bordered by Myanmar to the northwest, Laos to the northeast, Cambodia to the east, and Malaysia to the south.

Songkran Festival

Songkran" is the spring equinox in Sanskrit, which represents the arrival of the new year.It symbolizes the elimination of evil, misfortune, etc. Everyone will splash water on each other to bless each other to celebrate the coming of the New Year.

Loi Krathong Festival

It is known as "the most romantic festival"! A series of celebration and blessing activities will be held. The activities on the day of the full moon are the most abundant and exciting, including the release of water lanterns, sky lanterns, and temples. Lighting of lamps and prayers, float parade, fireworks ceremony.

Ten Thousand Buddhas Festival

Royal family members will personally participate in the blessing ceremony, and people will bring flowers, incense candles and offerings to nearby temples to carry out activities such as burning incense, and worshiping Buddha. In the evening, Three rounds of candle patrol ceremonies are held outside the main hall of the temple.
