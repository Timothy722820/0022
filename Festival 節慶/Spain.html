Fallas 法雅節

Every year on March 19th, in order to celebrate the birthday of the local patron saint, San José, different art works will be displayed on the streets. Except for one of the Fallas, which will be preserved and displayed in the museum, the rest All will be burned with fire. This is the most famous Las Fallas festival in Valencia every year, also commonly known as the Fire Festival.


Running of the Bulls 奔牛節

The Running of the Bulls is an event held in parts of Spain, southern France and Mexico, which began in 1591. The original purpose was to drive the bulls to the bullfighting ring, and the bulls were usually slaughtered after the day's bullfighting.
